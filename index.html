What i do

Services

SEO

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is the process of optimizing a website's technical setup, content relevancy, and link popularity so that its pages become more readily findable, relevant, and popular in response to user search queries, and, as a result, search engines rank them higher.

SEM (PPC)

Pay-Per-Click (PPC) is a type of Search Engine Marketing (SEM) focusing on paid ads. Advertisers place their ads on platforms like Google Ads, Bing Ads, or social media and only pay when users click on their ads, making it cost-effective and targeted.

SMM

Social media marketing promotes products or services on platforms and websites. While e-marketing and digital marketing remain common academically, social media marketing is growing in popularity for its effectiveness and reach among professionals and researchers.

Email Marketing

Email marketing involves sending commercial messages to groups via email, including ads, business requests, and sales or donation solicitations. Any email to potential or current customers is considered email marketing.

Content Marketing

Content marketing attracts, engages, and retains audiences by creating and sharing relevant articles, videos, and podcasts. It builds expertise, boosts brand awareness, and keeps your business in customers' minds when they buy.
